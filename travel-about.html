About Us

Traveldatediscover Page celebrates life by enabling like minded people to connect while traveling, highlighting the travel occasion, explore trending places, discover the world when we connect, and possibly finding someone or a group to travel together and explore the world.

Founded in 2015, Traveldatediscover's mission is to give people the power to share and make the world more open and connected without boundaries. People use traveldatediscover to find out what is going on in new places, and discover hidden gems in their own back yard, discovering like minded people with similar lifestyles, activities and personalities. People also use the Travel Diaries Page to stay connected with other explorers by sharing pictures of their adventures.
